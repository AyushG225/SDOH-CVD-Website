Ayush Ghosh

Ayush Ghosh is a senior at Bellarmine College Preparatory. He is passionate about social determinants and the intersection between computer science and health equity. Over the past few years, he has done several projects in the area of cardiovascular health and computer science, including "Predicting Influences of Social Determinants of Health on Cardiovascular Disease Susceptibility Using Machine Learning" and "Minimizing Discriminatory Bias in Healthcare Models Through Optimal Transportation and Threshold-Agnostic Fairness." These projects have won multiple awards at the Synopsys Science Fair, including 1st and 2nd awards in their categories.
